About Us

We introduce our self as Hi-tech, we are Udaipur Rajasthan based company. We are mainly involved in providing printing solutions. We are well equipped with state of art machinery for printing of paper, flex, vinyl etc. In paper printing we have complete set-up right from pre-press to post-press as well as for indoor and outdoor printing we have Solvent printer, Eco-solvent printer and Digital Printer.
